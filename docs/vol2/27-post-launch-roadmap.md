## SECTION 27: POST-LAUNCH ROADMAP  
  
### 27.1 — Season 1: "Цветочные Поляны" (Launch — Month 3)  
  
- 10 characters, 1 map, 20 tasks, 4 sabotages.  
- 50-tier Battle Pass.  
- Single-player + Multiplayer (4-8).  
- Daily challenges.  
- 50 hats, 10 pets.  
  
### 27.2 — Season 2: "Зимний Двор" (Month 4-6)  
  
- Winter map variation (snow, ice physics — sliding).  
- 2 new characters (Игорь-Дальнобойщик, Света-Блогерша-Здоровья).  
- 5 new tasks (winter-themed: "Почистить снег", "Нарядить ёлку").  
- New sabotage: "Гололёд" (everyone slides, movement impaired).  
- 50 new cosmetics.  
  
### 27.3 — Season 3: "Ночной Двор" (Month 7-9)  
  
- Night map variation (limited vision, streetlights create light pools).  
- 2 new characters (Ночной-Охранник, Подросток-Вейпер).  
- 5 new tasks (night-themed: "Поймать енота", "Закрыть гараж").  
- New sabotage: "Отключили свет" (total darkness, only flashlights).  
- New role: "Свидетель" (Witness — sees the last action of a dead player).  
  
### 27.4 — Season 4: "Дачный Посёлок" (Month 10-12)  
  
- New map: Dacha settlement (larger, more hiding spots, garden plots).  
- 2 new characters (Дачник-Николай, Бабушка-Зоя).  
- 5 new tasks (dacha-themed: "Полить огурцы", "Наколоть дров").  
- New sabotage: "Прорвало колодец" (water flood).  
- Vehicles: Bicycles (fast travel between points).  
  
### 27.5 — Long-Term Vision (Year 2+)  
  
- **Custom Maps:** Community-created maps (via a simple tilemap editor).  
- **Tournaments:** Weekly tournaments with real fuel ticket prizes.  
- **Esports:** Spectator mode for competitive play.  
- **Franchise:** Standalone mobile app (beyond Telegram), Steam release.  
- **Merch:** Physical "95-Й" merchandise (canister-shaped flasks, "Я не Сливщик" t-shirts).
